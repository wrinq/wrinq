1. Everything is stored in the browser including: the messages you send, the messages you recieve, your profile and the profile of your contacts
  2. If you are offline the messages sent to you are kept in database on server
  3. When you are available they are forwarded to you and deleted from database.
  4. If there are any images they are are displayed and links are made clickable. New lines are preserved. No other formatting is supported.
  5. You can filter messages by contact or by tag simply by clicking on the name of the contact or tag
  6. You can also search the name of contact by typing @name in search bar and tag by typing #tag in the search bar
  7. You can page through messages. By default the page size is 20
  8. Since the data is always local wrinq is very fast.
