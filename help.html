How wrinq can help your your business

Wrinq was designed with a goal of helping people to build communities. As a blogger, small business owner or a freelancer you know the importance of carving out a niche. Wrinq helps you create a community around your expertise so that you can engage with your readers, customers or clients.

The best part of all this is that wrinq is open source so you can host it on your own server. Even on a low end server wrinq gives you an appreciable performance.
