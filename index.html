With wrinq you get

Automatic email reminders

Wrinq will send periodic reminders to your tenants as the due date draws near

No sign up fees

wrinq is free to join. We take only a small transaction fee when your tenant pays you. We get paid when you get paid.

Rent history for tenants

As a tenant any rent that you pay to your landlord with wrinq will be recorded. You can view your payment history and even download old rent slips any time. All your payment history is tied to the email address on which the due rent notifications are sent.

Consolidated rent collections

With wrinq you have all your rent collections in one place. You can view your collection history, monthly and yearly logs, collections from a particular tenant and even the rent slips issued to them.

The other benefits

With wrinq we will help you keep your rental accounts in a tip top condition and help you avoid some common causes of disputes by automating bookkeeping for you.

We understand that managing a property is no easy task. Which is why we will be working closely with agents to help you with other aspects like: finding new tenants, helping you with their verification and with your tenancy agreements. Our mission is to make your rental business as smooth as we can :)

100% safe transactions via paypal

Paypal is the easiest and the most secure way to send and receive money online. It is trusted by millions of users and business owners worldwide. Paypal will take care of processing the money paid by your tenant and then we will direct it to securely deposit it into your account. After which you decide what to do with it. The process is instantaneous,safe and easy.

Automatic rent slips

wrinq automatically issues rent slips on behalf of landlord on successful payment of rent.
