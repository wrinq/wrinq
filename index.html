With wrinq you get

Automatic email reminders

Wrinq will send periodic reminders to your tenants when the due date draws near.

No sign up fees

wrinq is free to join. We take only a small transaction fee when your tenant pays you. We get paid when you get paid.

Rent history for tenants

As a tenant you can view your payment history and even download old rent slips at any time. All of your payments are tied to the email address on which the due rent notifications are sent.

Accept rent via a credit card

With wrinq you make your property more attractive by accepting a more convenient mode of payment. Your tenants will be able to pay their rent via a credit card.

Consolidated rent collections

With wrinq you have all of your rent collections in one place. You can view your collection history, your monthly and yearly logs, your collections from a particular tenant and even the rent slips issued to them.

Better accounts, less disputes

wrinq will keep your rental accounts in a tip top condition and help you avoid disputes which may arise due to sloppy accounting.

Help when you need it

Managing a property is no easy task. Which is why we will be partnering with agents to help you with other aspects of your business like finding new tenants, dealing with their verification and drafting tenancy agreements. Our mission is to make your rental business as smooth as we can :)

100% safe transactions via paypal

Paypal is the easiest and the most secure way to send and receive money online. It is trusted by millions of users and business owners worldwide. Paypal will process the rent paid by your tenant and then we will direct it to securely deposit it into your account. The process is instantaneous, safe and easy.

Automatic rent slips

wrinq will automatically issue rent slips on your behalf once your tenant pays the rent.

A platform for busy people

wrinq is designed in way that you can set and forget it. It will send you email notifications at appropriate intervals. So you won't need to fiddle around with it.
